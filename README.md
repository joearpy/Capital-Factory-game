## Capital Factory - economic game

I learned Computer Science at Collage of Kecskemét, Hungary. I finished at 2016. This was my degree thesis. I was developing a online game, this name is Capital Factory. This game playing at the factory, and the player is the director of this factory. The decions of the player is affect the future of the company.

## Technology

PHP, AJAX with jQuery, SQL, HTML, CSS
